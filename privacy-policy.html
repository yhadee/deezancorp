Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Deezan Corp ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website deezancorp.com and builds operational digitization systems and custom software. This Privacy Policy clarifies how we gather, process, transfer, and protect user data when utilizing our services.

We construct all applications to comply with the Google Play Developer Program Policies, including the Data Safety sections, and global frameworks like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our website or applications, we may collect the following category blocks:

A. Personal Information

Information provided voluntarily during contact requests, newsletter subscriptions, or account registrations, including name, corporate email address, business telephone number, and payment processing details.

B. Device and Usage Data

Automatic telemetry details collected via user visits, such as IP addresses, browser specifications, OS types, device models, screen resolutions, and click paths.

C. Mobile Permissions

Our applications may request specific system access to run correctly. You can disable these at any time via your device settings:

  • Biometrics Access: Required to verify identities locally on-device for secure login screens. We never upload your biometric data to our cloud servers.
  • Storage Permissions: Needed to cache local DB records or save offline assets.
  • Camera / Gallery: Utilized exclusively to allow profile pictures or barcode scans.

3. Third-Party Software Kits (SDKs)

To monitor performance and optimize application execution, our mobile products integrate secure third-party components. These services compile data independently under their respective privacy practices:

  • Google Play Services: Powers essential background API functions on Android.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Used to track app stability, identify bug triggers, and trace app crashes.
  • Stripe: Secure third-party handler for encrypted transaction payloads.

4. How We Use Your Data

We process collected data exclusively to:

  • Maintain, monitor, and optimize our applications' codebase.
  • Respond to technical support or general business inquiries.
  • Validate identity layers and prevent unauthorized login attempts.
  • Comply with developer guidelines, tax codes, and legal sub-warrants.

5. Data Security

All payload traffic in transit is protected using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) and Transport Layer Security (TLS) certificates. Storage buckets are encrypted-at-rest. We restrict data access only to authorized systems engineers under strict confidentiality contracts.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion requests

Depending on your jurisdiction, you hold the right to access, rectify, or request the complete deletion of your compiled personal records. We provide a direct process for users to delete their account data:

To request account deletion or export a copy of your records, contact our Compliance Officer at yhadee@gmail.com or visit our Contact Page. We will process requests within 30 days of validation.

7. Policy Updates

We update this privacy document occasionally to align with shifting mobile OS permissions or developer store rules. The date of change will always be displayed at the top.
