Who We Are

About Deezan Corp

A specialized provider of office and IT project outsourcing solutions, dedicated to helping companies digitize operations and deploy vetted remote teams.

Streamlining Operations Through Strategic Project Outsourcing

Founded to bridge the gap between complex office workflows and modern technological efficiency, Deezan Corp provides highly structured project outsourcing. We focus heavily on producing systems that automate manual procedures, digitize data operations, and deliver compliant business software.

Whether helping a corporation transition from spreadsheets to robust custom database portals or providing a dedicated remote engineering team to deliver software milestones, our workflow is driven by transparency and delivery excellence.

Our Mission

To empower organizations by providing reliable, secure, and highly efficient office and IT project outsourcing models that maximize operational velocity.

Our Vision

To become a regional benchmark for operational project outsourcing, recognized for transparent staffing integration and high-quality software craftsmanship.

Our History

Milestones & Company Timeline

A brief history of our evolution from a niche outsourcing consultancy to a leading corporate services provider.

2021

Founding & Core Focus

Deezan Corp was established in Jakarta with a focus on building custom spreadsheet automations and providing office operational assistance for local corporate clients.

2023

Expansion to Custom Portals

Expanded services to offer custom CRM/ERP portal design, database setup, and administrative workflow digitization. Successfully completed 25+ major project integrations.

2025

Dedicated Team Allocation

Launched our dedicated IT staffing division, supplying remote developers, operations admins, and support teams to streamline client projects.

Beyond
